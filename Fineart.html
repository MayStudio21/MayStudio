Printing

I first learned printmaking during my first year in Ireland, and since then, it has remained a passion of mine. I've enjoyed experimenting with various techniques, from lino prints to woodcuts, and even exploring screen printing. Each new method offers a fresh perspective, and I continue to experiment, always discovering new ways to express my creativity through print.
